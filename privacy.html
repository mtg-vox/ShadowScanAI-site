Privacy Policy

Effective: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information ShadowScan AI ("we," "us," "our") collects when you visit getshadowscan.com or use our services, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Who we are

ShadowScan AI is a website risk-scanning service operated by Bryan Totty (sole proprietor) in the United States. You can reach us at ShadowScanAI@proton.me.

2. Information we collect

  • Information you give us: name, email address, the website URL you submit for scanning, and any other details you send through forms or email.
  • Automatically collected: IP address, device and browser type, pages viewed, referring URL, and approximate location, collected through standard server logs and analytics.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see Section 6.

3. How we use information

  • To deliver the scan or service you requested.
  • To send you scan results, updates, and related communications.
  • To operate, secure, and improve the website and service.
  • To measure marketing performance, including ads on Meta and other platforms.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

4. Legal bases (EU/UK visitors)

If you are in the EU/UK, we rely on the following legal bases under the GDPR: contract (to provide the scan you requested), legitimate interests (to operate, secure, and improve our service), consent (for non-essential cookies and marketing pixels), and legal obligation where applicable.

5. How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We share limited data with service providers who help us run the business, including:

  • Formspree — processes form submissions.
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — ad measurement and optimization. Disable via the cookie banner.
  • Google Fonts — serves the page font from Google's CDN.
  • Hosting and email providers — to deliver the website and respond to you.
  • Legal authorities — if required by law.

6. Cookies and tracking

We use a small number of cookies and similar technologies. Essential cookies enable basic site function. The Meta Pixel and similar marketing tools only run after you accept cookies via the banner. You can change your choice anytime by clearing your browser storage for this site.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port the personal information we hold about you, and to opt out of certain processing such as targeted advertising or "sale/sharing" under California law. To exercise these rights email ShadowScanAI@proton.me. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

8. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Form submissions are typically retained for up to 24 months.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

10. International transfers

We are based in the United States. If you access the service from outside the US your information will be transferred to and processed in the US.

11. Children

The service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective" date above will be updated when changes are made. Material changes will be communicated through the website.

13. Contact

Questions or requests? Email ShadowScanAI@proton.me.
